## What Admin can do
- [ ] CRUD Vehicles
- [ ] Assign Vehicles to Drivers
- [ ] Schedule Maintenance for Vehicles
- [ ] Assign Maintenance Tasks to Mechanics
- [ ] View Reports and Analytics
- [ ] Blacklist Drivers or Mechanics
- [ ] Manage User Roles and Permissions

## What Drivers can do
- [ ] View Assigned Vehicles
- [ ] Update Vehicle Status (e.g., Available, In Use, Under Maintenance)
- [ ] Report Issues or Maintenance Needs
- [ ] View Maintenance History of Assigned Vehicles
- [ ] Update Personal Profile and Contact Information
- [ ] Log Driving Hours and Mileage
- [ ] Log Fuel Consumption and Expenses

## What Mechanics can do
- [ ] View Assigned Maintenance Tasks
- [ ] Update Maintenance Task Status (e.g., In Progress, Completed)
- [ ] Log Maintenance Details and Parts Used
- [ ] View Maintenance History of Vehicles
- [ ] Update Personal Profile and Contact Information
- [ ] Log Work Hours and Availability
- [ ] Request Additional Parts or Tools Needed for Maintenance Tasks
- [ ] View Reports on Maintenance Tasks and Vehicle Performance
